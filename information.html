Information

Welcome to Science Museum, a vibrant science museum for kids in the heart of the city!

Nestled in the heart of the city, our museum is a vibrant hub of exploration, captivating visitors of all ages. Step into a world where interactive exhibits bring science to life, from the marvels of physics and chemistry to the mysteries of space and time. Engage young minds with hands-on workshops, interactive demonstrations, and immersive displays that spark a love for discovery. With a commitment to education and entertainment, Science Museum is not just a museum—it's a gateway to the wonders of science, inspiring generations to explore and learn.
