Welcome to Science Museum, a vibrant science museum for kids in the heart of the city!

Dive into a world of wonder with interactive exhibits on physics, chemistry, biology, and astronomy. Build robots in Robotics Alley, explore ecosystems in Nature Nook, and journey through time in the Dino Discovery Zone. Ignite curiosity today!

With our special events you can bring your loved ones for a great time. Enjoy the wonders of our 3D-Museum, ignite the curiosity in your child and create memories that for a lifetime

The Dino Discovery Zone takes you on a captivating journey through time, unraveling the story of dinosaurs. From physics allure to thrilling chemistry experiments, Science Museum ignites curiosity and transforms learning into an adventure.
