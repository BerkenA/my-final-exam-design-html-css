Tickets

Dive into a world of options with Science Museum's diverse ticket offerings!

Choose from general admission for access to captivating exhibits, or elevate your experience with special packages, including interactive workshops and behind-the-scenes tours. Explore, learn, and make memories with our tailored ticket options for every curious explorer!

You can book your tickets online! Pay and recieve them when you arrive!

Young Explorer's Pass:

Ignite curiosity with our Young Explorer's Pass. Kids aged 7-15 enjoy unlimited access to all exhibits, ensuring a day filled with hands-on science exploration and interactive fun. Come enjoy with your family!

Adventure workshop:

Enhance the experience with Adventure Workshop Access. This ticket includes entry to all exhibits plus participation in special workshops designed for young minds eager to delve deeper into the wonders of science.
